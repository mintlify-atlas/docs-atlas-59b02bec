## Runtime requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|---|---|
| **macOS version** | macOS Monterey 12.0 or later |
| **Supported hardware** | Apple Silicon and Intel-based Macs |

Mist runs on any Mac that supports macOS Monterey or later. Both Apple Silicon (arm64) and Intel (x86\_64) architectures are fully supported.

## Hardware-specific capabilities

What Mist can download depends on the architecture of the Mac it is running on.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Apple Silicon" icon="microchip">
    Downloads **IPSW Firmware Restore** files (`.ipsw`) for Apple Silicon Macs. SHA-1 checksum validation is performed automatically on every download.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Intel-based" icon="computer">
    Generates macOS Installers in any combination of `.app`, `.dmg`, `.iso`, and `.pkg` formats. Chunklist checksum validation is performed on every download.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  On Apple Silicon Macs, Mist can also download **Universal** macOS Installers (for macOS Big Sur 11 and later) in all four export formats — not just Firmware files.
</Note>

## Full Disk Access

Certain operations — particularly downloading macOS Firmwares and generating Installer exports — require Mist to have **Full Disk Access** on your Mac. Without this permission, downloads or export operations may fail silently or with an error.

To grant Full Disk Access:

1. Open **System Settings > Privacy & Security > Full Disk Access**.
2. Enable the toggle next to **Mist**.
3. If prompted, authenticate with your administrator password.

<Warning>
  If Mist is not listed under Full Disk Access, launch Mist first and then attempt a download. Mist will prompt you to open the correct System Settings pane automatically.
</Warning>

## Privileged Helper Tool

Some export types — such as generating Bootable Disk Images (`.iso`) and macOS Installer Packages (`.pkg`) — require elevated privileges to run system-level commands. Mist installs an optional **Privileged Helper Tool** (`com.ninxsoft.mist.helper`) to handle these operations securely without requiring you to enter your password repeatedly.

You will be prompted to install the Privileged Helper Tool the first time you attempt an operation that requires it. Administrator credentials are required to complete the installation.

## Build requirements (developers only)

If you are building Mist from source rather than using a pre-built release, you need:

| Requirement | Version |
|---|---|
| **Swift** | 5.10 |
| **Xcode** | 15.0 |

Clone the repository from [github.com/ninxsoft/Mist](https://github.com/ninxsoft/Mist) and open `Mist.xcodeproj` in Xcode to build.
